Play Times

Wednesdays at 8:30pm in De Neve Dungeon!

Don't know where De Neve Dungeon is? Go to the regular entrance to De Neve dining hall, but take a right and walk until you see a set of double doors in front of a study lounge. You might have to knock to get in because it can be locked. Behind it is the De Neve Dungeon where 1 of the pool tables are! The other one is upstairs to the left of the De Neve front desk, but we only go there if the first table is unavailable or full.

Tournaments

We have tournaments around every quarter

There is usually a $5 buy-in and we play a full tournament of matches according to official 8-ball pool rules. Usually 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place gets monetary rewards. Join us!
